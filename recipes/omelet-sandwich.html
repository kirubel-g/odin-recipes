Triple Egg Meat & Cheese Sandwich

Description

This breakfast meal is a great source of macronutrients. This nearly 1,300 calorie meal is sure to give anyone enough energy to conquer their day! You will need:

  • 3 eggs
  • 2 servings of pepper jack cheese
  • 2 slices of bread
  • 8 slices of chicken meat
  • Milk (optional)

The steps are as follows:

  1. Apply non-stick spray or butter to a pan and simmer
  2. As the pan is simmering, put 8 slices of chicken meat on a slice of bread
  3. Put one slice of cheese on the meat, and another on the other slice of bread
  4. Toast both slices of bread in a toaster oven set to "Medium"
  5. Crack 3 eggs and put them on the pan
    • Optional: Crack 3 eggs in a bowl and a bit of milk. Stir before placing on pan
  6. After eggs are done cooking, put the omelet on the sandwhich
Voila! Enjoy an amazing breakfast paired with a Chocolate Milkshake.
