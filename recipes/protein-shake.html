Chocolate Milkshake

Description

I use this meal as a quick way to ingest a lot of my daily macronutrients, mostly protein and carbohydrates. The ingredients are as follows:

  • 1.5 cups of whole milk
  • 1 cup of original Quakers Oats
  • 2 tbsp of peanut butter
  • 1 scoop of Chocolate flavored Whey Protein Powder
  • 1 medium-sized, peeled banana

Will need a blender to make meal. Directions are as follows:

  1. Pour the following, in sequential order, in a 32 oz blender cup:
    1. Oats
    2. Milk
    3. Peanut Butter
    4. Protein Powder
    5. Banana
  2. Blend for 30 seconds
  3. Enjoy!
